Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at Badri Traders. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for an account and use the Badri Traders app, we collect the following personal information that you voluntarily provide to us:

  • Contact Data: Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Delivery Data: Shipping and billing addresses required to fulfill your orders.
  • Authentication Data: Passwords (encrypted) and Google Sign-In tokens.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information collected via our App for a variety of business purposes described below:

  • To facilitate account creation and logon process.
  • To fulfill and manage your orders, payments, and deliveries.
  • To send administrative information to you, such as order confirmations or app updates.

3. Will Your Information Be Shared?

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services (such as secure database hosting via Supabase), or to fulfill business obligations. We do not sell your personal data to third-party marketers.

4. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We keep your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy notice unless otherwise required by law. You can request the deletion of your account and data at any time through the app's settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this notice, you may email us at support@badritraders.com.
